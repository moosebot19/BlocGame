BLOC — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

BLOC ("the Game") is a block puzzle game developed by Yousef Mousa. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Information: If you create an account, we store your chosen nickname and a hashed version of your PIN. We never store your PIN in plain text. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from the provider to create your account.

Game Data: We store your game scores, line counts, and game mode for the leaderboard. This data is associated with your account.

Friends: If you add friends, we store the connection between your account and theirs.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, browsing history, contacts, photos, or any data beyond what is listed above. We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not serve advertisements.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide game features: displaying your scores on leaderboards, maintaining your account, and connecting you with friends. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (powered by PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. Data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will permanently delete your account, scores, and friend connections.

Children's Privacy

BLOC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game is designed for general audiences.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
